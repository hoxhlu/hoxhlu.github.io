Big Foot Slab

Inspired by western slab types, this type has very unique shape as its feet are larger than the rest of its much thinner body. Which is different to a normal western slab that is both top and bottom heavy. The name came from the fact it has very large feet and reminds me of big foot. As big foot leaves his mark where ever he goes simalar to my type leaving it't foot print where ever web screen or poster it goes.
